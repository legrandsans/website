Alone.

Alone. is a horror game on Roblox inspired by DOORS (another game on Roblox created by the LSPLASH team).

It's a rogue-lite where you have to open doors to reach the final room, A1000. However, there are entities lurking around. We recommend playing it with as little prior knowledge as possible.
